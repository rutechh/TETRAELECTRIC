Single Phase Meter Enclosure

ME13 Single phase meter enclosure is specially designed for the purpose of anti-tamper and protection of meter. One DIN rail type meter and one 2P MCB can be mounted with bottom connection. The material of enclosure is polycarbonate with UV resistance and V0 frame resistance degree. Pole mounted or wall mounted or lock and key protection are based on project demand. Alarm sensor for enclosure opening is reserved function.
